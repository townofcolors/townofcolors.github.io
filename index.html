Town of Colors

Helping Create Color in Northern Iowa

An Initiative Determined to Inspire Curiosity and Creativity

Town Of Colors is an initiative determined to inspire curiosity and creativity using art. We want to color the city's of Northern Iowa using local artists and creative thinkers. We are an outbound based initiative from Iowa BIG, we want to host various artists throughout cities at local businesses. Town of Colors also wants to bring mural and street art to Northern Iowa.
